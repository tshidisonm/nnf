About Ntodeni Nenungwi Foundation

The Ntodeni Nenungwi Foundation (NNF) is committed to empowering learners in the Vhembe East District, Limpopo. We work in partnership with the Limpopo Provincial Government, Department of Education, and corporate sponsors like Boxer.

Our mission: keep less privileged learners in school, celebrate academic excellence, and inspire purpose in every child.

Chairman: Ntodeni Nenungwi

Focus: Back to School campaigns, Grade 12 Excellence Awards, Youth empowerment, and leadership development.

“The youth are not just the future – they are the change we need today.” – Ntodeni Nenungwi Foundation
